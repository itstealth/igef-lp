Indo Global Group of Colleges is a group of self-financed, unaided private institutes established in 2003 by the Indo Global Education Foundation (IGEF). Strategically located at Indo Global Education City in Abhipur, Mohali — just 5 km from Chandigarh — the group comprises four specialised colleges offering programs in Engineering, Architecture, Management, and Education.

Affiliated with IKG Punjab Technical University (PTU) and approved by AICTE, CoA, and NCTE, the institution has consistently set benchmarks in academics, industry interface, and student placements. With an on-campus IBM Centre of Excellence, live industry projects, and 150+ recruiting partners, IGEF bridges the gap between classroom learning and real-world careers.

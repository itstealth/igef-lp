Student Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about admissions, fees, scholarships, and campus life—answered by our team.

Candidates must have passed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination from a recognised board with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in relevant subjects. For engineering programmes, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics are mandatory. Some specialised courses may also require a valid entrance test score (JEE / university entrance). Merit-based admissions open every year in June. View detailed eligibility ›

Visit our official portal and click "Apply Now". You'll need: 10th & 12th mark sheets, a recent passport-size photograph, government-issued ID proof, transfer certificate from your previous institution, and your entrance test scorecard (if applicable). Upload all documents in PDF/JPG format (max 2 MB each). Start your application ›

Applications open 1 March 2025 and close 30 May 2025. Merit lists are published on 15 June. Admission and fee payment window: 16–30 June. Classes commence from 1 August 2025. Late admissions (subject to availability) may be considered up to 31 July. Download academic calendar ›

Yes. Lateral entry and credit transfer are facilitated under the National Credit Framework (NCrF). A maximum of 50% of total programme credits may be transferred. Your transcripts will be evaluated by the Academic Equivalency Committee within 10 working days of submission. Contact the registrar ›

Tuition fees range from ₹80,000 to ₹1,60,000 per annum depending on the programme. The fee covers academic instruction, library access, basic laboratory usage, and student welfare activities. Hostel, transport, and examination fees are charged separately. A detailed fee schedule is available on the finance portal. View fee structure ›

We offer merit-cum-means scholarships covering up to 100% of tuition fees for eligible students. Government scholarships (NSP, state portal) are also facilitated. Sports, cultural achievers, and students from economically weaker sections receive special waivers. Applications open alongside admissions and require income certificate, previous academic records, and a personal statement. Explore scholarships ›

Yes. Our Finance Cell has tie-ups with SBI, Bank of Baroda, and Axis Bank for hassle-free education loans at preferential rates. A dedicated liaison officer assists with documentation, bank coordination, and moratorium period queries. Visit the Finance Cell, Ground Floor, Admin Block, or email finance@institution.edu.in.

Separate, fully-furnished hostels are available for boys and girls on campus with 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, RO water, and a modern dining hall. Hostel allocation is on a first-come, first-served basis after fee payment. We have 2,400+ residential seats across 8 blocks. Outstation students are given priority. Hostel brochure ›

Our campus hosts 40+ student clubs spanning robotics, debate, photography, classical arts, coding, entrepreneurship, and more. Sports facilities include a 400-metre track, swimming pool, cricket ground, and indoor courts. The annual cultural fest "Utsav" and tech fest "Ignite" draw participants from across the country. Student activities portal ›

A fully-equipped Health Centre with a resident doctor and nursing staff operates 24×7 on campus. Free OPD consultations, first-aid, and ambulance services are available to all students. A dedicated Student Counselling Cell offers confidential mental health support and career guidance sessions every weekday. Health centre contacts ›

The 2024 batch recorded a 94% placement rate with an average CTC of ₹8.2 LPA. Top recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Deloitte, Amazon, Microsoft, and over 300 other companies. The highest package offered was ₹42 LPA (international). Our Training & Placement Cell offers year-round aptitude coaching, mock interviews, and industry mentorship. View placement report ›

Yes. Internship opportunities are facilitated from the 2nd year onwards through our industry partners. Summer internships (6–8 weeks) are mandatory for engineering students. Many students convert internships into pre-placement offers (PPOs). The placement portal lists live internship openings throughout the year. Browse internships ›
